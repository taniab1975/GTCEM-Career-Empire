Career Empire takes Year 12 Careers and Employability and turns it into something students can actually get into.

Instead of another worksheet about the future, they make choices, take on challenges, earn rewards, and watch their career profile grow.

Megatrends is where the big decisions come in: technology, climate, demographics, and global change. Every choice has trade-offs for salary, lifestyle, job security, and skill growth.

The student hub keeps it all together, showing progress, evidence, purchases, recent activity, and what to tackle next.

Modules like Lifelong Learning and EST Prep feed back into the same profile, so it feels like one journey, not a pile of separate tasks.

There is a class layer too. Students build assets, contribute community tax, vote on causes, and see shared impact.

For teachers, the dashboard turns gameplay into useful signals: who is progressing, who needs support, and what evidence is ready to discuss.

The goal is pretty simple: careers learning that feels active for students, and visible for teachers.
