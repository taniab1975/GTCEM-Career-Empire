Not on an approved email domain?

If you're a teacher and your email address doesn't end in `@cewa.edu.au` or `.wa.edu.au`, please email tania.byrnes@cewa.edu.au to add your email to the list of eligible registrations.

Allowed domains

Allowed: `cewa.edu.au`, any domain ending in `.wa.edu.au`

Blocked: personal email providers and student-style addresses

Why this matters

It keeps teacher access restricted to verified school staff and protects class management features.
