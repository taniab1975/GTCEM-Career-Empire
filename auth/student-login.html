Student Access

Log in with the username your teacher created for you.

Students do not use email addresses in this system. Use the exact username your teacher created for you.

Username rules

Use: the exact teacher-issued username

Examples: DiegoF, CarysMJ, MarkT, MarkTh1

Not allowed: student email addresses or full email-style usernames

Why no student email?

Teachers control class setup and student accounts. This keeps the platform simpler, safer, and more classroom-friendly.
