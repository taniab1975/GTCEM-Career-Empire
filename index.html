Related Vocabulary from SCSA Glossary of Terms

Megatrend: A significant & long-lasting development that has a transformational impact on the way we live, work and do business

Demographic Shift: The change in the size and nature of a population due to migration and changes in birth and death rates.

Economic Power Shift: The long-term global shift away from established advanced economies to emerging market countries.

Impactful technologies: The emergence of technologies that have the capacity to improve products, services and processes, but to also cause disruption and uncertainty for society and the economy.
